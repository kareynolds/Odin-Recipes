Easy Slow Cooker Soup

Cheesy Potato Soup

Rich, thick and creamy, this soup is a great way to use up leftover holiday ham. Made in the slow cooker, this simple recipe allows use of quick tricks such as frozen hash brown potatoes. With very little effort this delicious soup is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1 28 ounce package frozen has brown potatoes thawed
  • 2 cups diced cooked ham
  • half cup diced onion
  • 2 stalks celery chopped
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • quarter teaspoon of salt
  • quarter teaspoon of pepper
  • quater teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 8 ounce package of cream cheese, softened
  • 1 8 ounce package of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onions, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Combine hash browns, ham, onion, celery, chicken broth, salt, pepper, and garlic powder in a slow cooker. Stir well to combine. Place the lid on the slow cooker.
  2. Cook on Low heat until celery is soft, 4 to 5 hours.
  3. Mix in cream cheese and Cheddar cheese.Serve topped with chopped green onions as a garnish. Stir until cheese is melted. Cover and cook on Low for an additional 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Serve topped with chopped green onions as a garnish.
